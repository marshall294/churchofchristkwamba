Lesson: The Ten Commandments

After leading the Israelites out of Egypt, God called Moses up to Mount Sinai. There, God gave him Ten Commandments — special rules for how to live and love God and others. These commandments are a gift that show us the best way to live according to God's will. When we obey them, we honor God and help create a kinder, better world!

  • God Prepares the People:

    Exodus 19:3-8: God tells Moses to prepare the Israelites for His special message.

  • Meeting God on the Mountain:

    Exodus 19:16-20: God's presence covers Mount Sinai with thunder, lightning, and smoke!

  • The Ten Commandments:

    Exodus 20:1-17: God gives Moses the Ten Commandments for all the people.

  • The Promise:

    Exodus 24:3-8: The people promise to follow all that God has commanded.

Assessment Questions

3-5 Years Old

  • 1. Who gave Moses the Ten Commandments?
  • 2. Where did Moses meet God?
  • 3. What are the Ten Commandments?
  • 4. Why do we follow God's rules?
  • 5. Name one of God's rules!
  • 6. Who helps us obey God's commandments today?

6-7 Years Old

  • 1. Why did God give us the Ten Commandments?
  • 2. What did the mountain look like when God came down?
  • 3. How do the Ten Commandments help us love others?
  • 4. What does it mean to honor your parents?
  • 5. How can we show we love God?
  • 6. Why is it important to tell the truth?

8-10 Years Old

  • 1. How do the first four commandments teach us to love God?
  • 2. How do the last six commandments teach us to love people?
  • 3. Why did God speak with thunder and lightning?
  • 4. How can we obey God's commandments in our daily lives?
  • 5. What happens when we break God's commandments?
  • 6. How does Jesus help us live according to God's will?

11-15 Years Old

  • 1. Why are the Ten Commandments still important today?
  • 2. What does the giving of the Law reveal about God's character?
  • 3. How do the commandments show the relationship between faith and actions?
  • 4. Why did the people promise to obey God?
  • 5. How can understanding the Ten Commandments deepen our walk with God?
  • 6. How can we rely on the Holy Spirit to live out God's will?
