"God’s Power Over All."

Lesson: The Ten Plagues

When Pharaoh refused to let the Israelites go, God showed His mighty power through ten terrible plagues. Each plague proved that God is more powerful than any king, god, or force on earth. Even though Pharaoh's heart was hard, God remained faithful to His people and prepared the way for their freedom. This story reminds us that nothing is greater than God, and He will always fight for His children.

  • The Warning: Moses tells Pharaoh to let the people go.

    Exodus 7:16: "Let my people go, so that they may worship me in the wilderness."

  • The First Plague: Water turns to blood.

    Exodus 7:20: Moses strikes the Nile, and it becomes blood.

  • More Plagues: Frogs, gnats, flies, livestock disease, boils, hail, locusts, darkness, and death of the firstborn.

    Exodus 8–12: God sends plague after plague, but Pharaoh refuses to listen until the final plague.

  • God’s Victory: Pharaoh finally lets the Israelites go.

    Exodus 12:31: "Up! Leave my people, you and the Israelites! Go, worship the Lord as you have requested."

Assessment Questions

3-5 Years Old

  • 1. Who did God send to talk to Pharaoh?
  • 2. What happened to the river water?
  • 3. Name one animal that was part of the plagues.
  • 4. Why did God send the plagues?
  • 5. What happened after the last plague?
  • 6. Who is more powerful than Pharaoh?

6-7 Years Old

  • 1. Why did Pharaoh refuse to let the Israelites go?
  • 2. How many plagues did God send?
  • 3. What were some of the plagues?
  • 4. What does this story show about God's power?
  • 5. How did Pharaoh react after each plague?
  • 6. What can we learn about trusting God?

8-10 Years Old

  • 1. How did each plague challenge Egypt’s false gods?
  • 2. Why do you think Pharaoh's heart was hard?
  • 3. How did God protect the Israelites during the plagues?
  • 4. What does this story teach about obedience?
  • 5. How does God's power work in our lives today?
  • 6. Why is it important to listen to God right away?

11-15 Years Old

  • 1. In what ways did the Ten Plagues demonstrate God's sovereignty?
  • 2. How did God show patience and justice through the plagues?
  • 3. What does Pharaoh’s stubbornness teach us?
  • 4. How do the plagues point to the greatness of God’s deliverance?
  • 5. Why is trusting God's timing important?
  • 6. How do we see God's power displayed in the world today?
