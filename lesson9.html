Crossing the Red Sea

"God Leads Us to Safety."

Lesson: Crossing the Red Sea

After escaping Egypt, the Israelites found themselves trapped between the Red Sea and Pharaoh's army. They were afraid, but Moses told them to trust God. God performed a miracle by parting the waters, making a dry path through the sea. The Israelites crossed safely, and the pursuing Egyptians were swept away. This amazing story reminds us that God leads us, protects us, and makes a way even when things seem impossible!

  • The Israelites Escape Egypt:

    Exodus 14:5-9: Pharaoh changes his mind and chases after the Israelites.

  • The People Are Afraid:

    Exodus 14:10-12: The Israelites cry out to Moses when they see the Egyptians coming.

  • God Makes a Way:

    Exodus 14:21-22: Moses stretches out his hand, and God parts the Red Sea.

  • Victory!

    Exodus 14:29-31: The Israelites safely walk through the sea, and they see God's great power.

Assessment Questions

3-5 Years Old

  • 1. Who was chasing the Israelites?
  • 2. What was in front of the Israelites?
  • 3. What did God do to the sea?
  • 4. Who led the Israelites across the sea?
  • 5. What happened to the Egyptian army?
  • 6. Who keeps us safe today?

6-7 Years Old

  • 1. Why were the Israelites afraid?
  • 2. What did Moses tell the people to do?
  • 3. How did God part the Red Sea?
  • 4. What lesson can we learn about trusting God?
  • 5. How did the Israelites know God was powerful?
  • 6. How can we trust God when we are scared?

8-10 Years Old

  • 1. How did the Israelites feel when they saw Pharaoh’s army?
  • 2. What does Moses’ faith teach us?
  • 3. Why do you think God led the people through the Red Sea?
  • 4. How did God protect His people during the crossing?
  • 5. What can we do when we face "impossible" situations?
  • 6. How does God show His power in our lives today?

11-15 Years Old

  • 1. What does the Red Sea crossing teach about God’s deliverance?
  • 2. How is God's power over nature shown here?
  • 3. What role did obedience play in the Israelites' rescue?
  • 4. How can this story encourage us during tough times?
  • 5. What happens when we trust God instead of giving into fear?
  • 6. How does God still lead His people to safety today?
